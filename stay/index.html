Why Stay With Us

A Stay Worth the Drive

When you book with us, you're not just reserving a bed — you're stepping into one of Colorado's oldest continuously storied properties, now fully restored to its original character and then some. We moved here brick-by-brick from Silver Cliff in 1883, and we've been making ourselves at home ever since. Your room is beautifully appointed. Your evenings are taken care of. Your mornings start right across the street. All you have to do is arrive.

  • 36 thoughtfully designed rooms in a landmark building, each curated with over 300 original historic photographs
  • Four on-property dining and drinking venues — from morning coffee at Standard Oil Coffee Bar to an intimate nightcap in The War Room speakeasy
  • A downtown Cañon City address, walkable to shops and galleries, with Royal Gorge Bridge & Park just 15 minutes away
  • The kind of hospitality we call unreasonable — attentive, personal, and genuinely glad you're here
