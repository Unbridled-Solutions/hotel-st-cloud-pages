🌐 The CCO's Role in the Grid

The CCO appears in every layer — not to manage the work, but to set the standard, champion the client, and orchestrate across brands. The gold dot means ownership of the outcome, not the execution. Presidents own execution. The CCO owns the promise.

🦌 Unbridled as the Core

Unbridled Solutions leads more cells than any other brand — because it is the original, the flagship, and the culture origin. But it is not a hierarchy. It is a hub. The other brands don't report to it. They collaborate with it.

🎁 Gift'd as the Delight Engine

Gift'd appears in more client-facing moments than any other spoke brand. Gifting is not a product. It's a signal — "we thought about you before you arrived." That signal, repeated at every touchpoint, is what makes clients feel seen.

✈️ Travel as the Invisible Infrastructure

Unbridled Travel does its best work when no one notices. The attendee lands, transfers, checks in, and moves through the experience without friction. That invisibility is the achievement. Travel shows up in almost every layer — quietly making everything else possible.

🎬 Productions + 🎥 Media: The Memory Makers

Productions creates the moment. Media preserves it. Together they ensure that what happens on-site echoes long after the event ends. A great event is felt once. A great event, well captured, is felt every time someone watches the recap.

📐 How to Read This Grid

Leads (gold) = owns the outcome for this function.
Contributes (blue) = active partner, material input.
Supports (green) = enables without owning.
Aware (~) = informed and aligned, not actively involved.
CCO (●) = orchestrates, champions, or sets the standard.
