Now Hiring Baristas: The Dual-Purpose Ad Strategy

Two approaches: recruit great baristas while marketing the coffee shop · For Stan's review
MC's Take — The Strategy

The hiring angle is a smart double play. When a specialty coffee shop signals it's looking for qualified baristas, it tells every coffee drinker who sees the ad: we take the craft seriously. It's not just a job posting — it's a credibility signal.

There are two ways to run this. Option A keeps the existing customer-focused ads exactly as approved — the orange bar at the top does all the hiring work. Option B builds a dedicated ad group targeting people actively searching for barista jobs. Different keywords, recruiter-first copy, same brand.

💡 Recommendation: Run both. Option A costs nothing extra — layers onto the existing $35/day. Option B adds $10–15/day and targets a completely different audience. Together they cover customers AND recruits without cannibalizing each other.
Option A — Existing Ads + Hiring Bar (zero extra budget)
The approved coffee copy stays exactly as-is. The orange bar runs across the top of each ad — the first thing anyone sees.
