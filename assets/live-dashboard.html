Analyst Notes — Week of May 26–June 1, 2026
Local Proximity & Through Traffic is the star this week — a 20.26% CTR is genuinely exceptional for search, and at $2.59 average CPC it's converting highway intent into real clicks efficiently. That said, it's eating 38% of total budget, so the single conversion logged needs to trend up before we can call the CPA healthy. Royal Gorge Basecamp is the quiet overachiever — nearly 5 conversions on $309 spend puts cost-per-conversion around $62, which is solid for hotel bookings; the mid-week click dip (May 28) is worth watching to see if it repeats. Bars and Bathrooms spiked hard on May 29 (44 clicks, $76 spend) and then pulled back — that Thursday pattern could be tied to weekend intent searches, so consider a bid boost Wednesday nights to capitalize. Standard Oil Coffee needs a look — two zero-click days in seven and a 2.53% CTR tells us the creative or targeting isn't connecting yet; with only $22.62 in spend it's low risk, but it's also low signal — time to test new ad copy before the campaign settles into underperformance.
