MC's Take
MC
MC — Campaign Analyst Rolling 7-Day Read · June 4, 2026

Local Proximity & Through Traffic is the star of the portfolio right now. A 19% CTR is legitimately exceptional for display/search — that's Highway 50 drive-market intent translating directly into clicks, and it's eating 42% of spend to produce the most conversions (3) at a reasonable $177 cost-per-conv. This campaign is earning its budget.

FP Sports Bar is the volume king (3,732 impressions, 210 clicks) but hasn't logged a single conversion this week. It has the lowest CPC in the portfolio at $1.17, which is great — but at some point impressions and clicks need to become butts in barstools. Worth a landing page audit or a conversion action check before adding budget.

Royal Gorge Basecamp and Bars & Bathrooms are performing solidly in the mid-tier — consistent clicks, reasonable CPCs, and a handful of conversions between them. Both hold their own. The Bars and Bathrooms daily data cuts off after May 31, which is worth flagging — verify the campaign didn't pause mid-week.

Standard Oil Coffee Co needs a conversation. 483 impressions, 14 clicks, 2.9% CTR, zero conversions — and it's only spending $29 total, which suggests it's budget-constrained or under-targeted. Either invest in it properly or pull back and redirect that budget to Local Proximity, which is clearly converting. Half-measures on ad spend just waste money slowly.
