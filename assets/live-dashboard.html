Rolling 7-Day · May 21–27, 2026

Royal Gorge Basecamp is the clear efficiency winner this week — it pulled 4.5 conversions at $85.37 each, the lowest cost-per-conversion in the portfolio, while spending only 24% of the total budget. That's the kind of ratio that deserves more fuel, not less.

Local Proximity & Through Traffic is eating 34.7% of spend and posting a blazing 20.47% CTR — people are clicking, but only 1.5 conversions to show for it. Something is happening between the click and the conversion. Worth checking the landing page experience for highway-intent visitors before we pour more budget in.

Bars and Bathrooms had its best days early in the week (41 clicks on the 22nd), then trailed off hard — 19 clicks on the 27th with spend falling proportionally. Weekend-heavy audience behaving as expected, but the Monday drop warrants watching. If it keeps sliding, pause and reassess ad fatigue.

Standard Oil Coffee went dark on the 26th — zero clicks, zero spend. Could be a budget cap hit or a scheduling gap. Either way it needs attention; a zero-spend day on a live campaign is a flag, not a feature.
