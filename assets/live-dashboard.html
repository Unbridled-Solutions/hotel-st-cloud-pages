Local Proximity & Through Traffic is the clear standout this week — a 20.95% CTR is exceptional for search, and it's pulling nearly half the portfolio spend while logging 3 conversions. That's your workhorse. Keep the budget there and don't touch what's working.

Royal Gorge Basecamp is spending $264 for 1 conversion and a solid 8.63% CTR — not bad, but the cost-per-conversion (~$264) is the number to watch. That needs attention: either it hit a budget cap early or there's a targeting/status issue that got missed. FP Sports Bar is steady and efficient at $1.19 avg CPC, but zero conversions on 209 clicks is a funnel problem worth diagnosing. Standard Oil is effectively in test mode at $24 spend — either scale it or pause it, because at this level you're not getting meaningful signal.
