Weekly Analysis

Local Proximity & Through Traffic is the star this week — a 20.3% CTR on highway-intent searches is legitimately great, and at $3.26 CPC it's earning every dollar of its 34% budget share. That campaign is doing exactly what it's supposed to do: pulling in drivers on Highway 50 who weren't already planning to stop. Keep feeding it. Bars and Bathrooms leads in volume (3,639 impressions, 5 conversions) and is cost-efficient at $1.68 CPC — the Speakeasy angle is clearly resonating. Royal Gorge Basecamp showed the best momentum of the week, with clicks and spend both climbing into the weekend, suggesting Memorial Day travel intent is building; worth watching closely for a budget bump opportunity. Standard Oil Coffee needs a creative review — zero clicks and zero spend on Sunday the 26th is a red flag, CTR is soft at 4%, and it's the only campaign with no conversions. The product is good; the ads might not be telling that story yet.
