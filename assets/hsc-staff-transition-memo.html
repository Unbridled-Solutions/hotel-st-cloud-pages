Team Memo
June 25, 2026

Team,

There's a lot happening right now, and I want to make sure you're hearing it from me directly. I know I don't always communicate as fast as I should when things are in motion, I'm working on that. These changes are significant, and you deserve to know what's going on.

Josh Elder

Josh Elder is no longer with Hotel St. Cloud, effective immediately.

Josh has real talent in the kitchen. That part I want to acknowledge. But talent alone isn't enough here. We're building something that's as much about how we treat each other as it is about what we put on a plate. When we got honest with ourselves, the culture fit just wasn't there. And at Unbridled, when we have to choose between culture and culinary, we choose culture. Every time. We wouldn't be who we say we are if we didn't.

We wish Josh well.

Two Promotions I'm Excited to Announce

Now for the part I've been looking forward to.

Lorin Tedesco is being promoted to Director of Hotel & Events. Lorin owns rooms, housekeeping, and events going forward. If you've worked alongside Lorin, you already know what I'm going to say. She has been here from the jump. She's handled the hard days and the good ones the same way, steady, focused, and genuinely caring about the people around her. This is a long time coming.

Jeremy Reith is being promoted to Director of Restaurants. Jeremy takes ownership of all Hotel St. Cloud restaurants and bars, including the kitchen. We've been talking for a long time about breaking down the wall between front of house and back of house, creating one unified team that's accountable to the same standards. Jeremy is the person who can make that real. He's built something special here. The guests feel it, and so does the team.

These two have been with us through thick and thin. They've earned this. Please take a moment to congratulate them.

What's Next

Over the next few months we'll evaluate the kitchen and figure out the right path forward, whether that's bringing on a chef, promoting someone already on our team into a chef or kitchen manager role, or something else. We'll take our time and get it right.

We're also exploring whether a Hotel General Manager makes sense as we continue to grow. More on that when we know more.

For now, Lorin and Jeremy are your leadership team. Trust them, communicate with them, and keep bringing what you've been bringing. That's what's going to make this place great.

I also want to take a moment to recognize Jessica Carter, our Assistant Controller. Jessica has been working tirelessly behind the scenes through all of these changes, creating accountability and visibility into our numbers so we can operate sustainably. Without her diligence and financial accuracy, we would be flying blind. She doesn't always get the credit she deserves, and I want to change that.

Grateful for every one of you,

Stan

Owner & Managing Member
