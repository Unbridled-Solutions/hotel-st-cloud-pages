How to use this model: Click any Revenue or EBITDA cell to enter real numbers. All figures in thousands (K). The model auto-saves to your browser so your numbers persist between visits.

Fee basis matters: "Gross Profit" = Revenue minus COGS. "EBITDA" = after operating expenses. "Revenue" = top-line. Your current 2% royalty is applied to gross profit per the existing structure.

Market context: Active family offices providing shared services, strategic oversight, and operational support typically charge 5–15% of EBITDA. PE holding companies often charge 1.5–2% of revenue plus management fees. Your current model is conservative — there's room.

Excluded companies (unchecked): UnbridledACTS is a 501(c)3 and excluded by default. Exclude any company not yet generating revenue or where a fee isn't appropriate.
