Style Guide — When to Use Each Setup
Style Density Best For
Rounds of 8 12 sq ft / person Galas, awards dinners, social events — full dining service, conversation-focused
Rounds of 10 11 sq ft / person Higher-capacity banquets — slightly tighter, works well for shorter meal functions
Theater 7 sq ft / person General sessions, keynotes, ceremonies — maximum heads in seats, no tables
Classroom 18 sq ft / person Training, breakouts, workshops — full writing surface, extended sessions
Chevron 20 sq ft / person General sessions with interaction — angled rows improve sightlines and engagement
Planning note: These calculations use approximately 80% of gross square footage to account for staging, AV, service aisles, registration tables, and egress requirements. Always verify with your venue operations team — fire code occupancy limits and specific room features (columns, service doors, pre-function areas) may affect actual capacity. For precise room diagrams, use a CAD layout tool.
