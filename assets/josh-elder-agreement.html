Within his first 90 days of employment, the Company documented multiple incidents involving Josh's interpersonal conduct with coworkers, management, and consultants. These incidents resulted in a formal complaint, observations from several members of the leadership team, and a direct conversation with ownership.

Section 3
30-Day Review Period

This Agreement establishes a 30-calendar-day review period beginning June 16, 2026 and concluding on July 16, 2026. During this period, Josh's conduct will be evaluated against the Required Behaviors defined in Section 4. Progress may be discussed informally at any point; the formal review occurs on July 16, 2026.

Section 4
Required Behaviors

The following behaviors are required throughout the 30-day review period. These are observable and specific — not aspirational. Meeting these standards is the baseline for continued employment.

  • 1
    Meeting attendance. Attends all scheduled meetings. Any cancellation must be communicated at least 24 hours in advance. The meeting must be rescheduled within 48 hours of cancellation.
  • 2
    Third party present. All meetings with ownership, management, or consultants require a neutral third party present. Josh is responsible for coordinating this. No one-on-one meetings with leadership or consultants.
  • 3
    Digital responsiveness. Responds to all text and email communications from leadership within 4 business hours. If unable to respond within that window, Josh must contact the sender by phone to acknowledge and provide an alternate response time.
  • 4
    No hostile conduct. Zero reports of hostile, intimidating, aggressive, or threatening behavior received from any coworker, supervisor, consultant, or guest. This includes formal complaints and credible informal observations reported to ownership.
  • 5
    Prepared for meetings. Arrives having reviewed the agenda and with any relevant data or materials ready for discussion. If prep materials are needed and not provided in advance, Josh flags this at least 24 hours before the meeting.
  • 6
    Weekly self-reflection. Submits one written self-reflection per calendar week to Stan Bullis. Format: what went well, what was challenging, one thing he is actively working on. No longer than one page. Due each Sunday by end of day.

Section 6
Review Date — July 16, 2026

A formal check-in will occur on July 16, 2026, to assess Josh's performance against Section 4.

✓ If behaviors are met

Section 7
Single-Incident Termination
Zero-Tolerance — Immediate Grounds for Termination

Notwithstanding the 30-day review period, a single serious incident of misconduct during the review period — including but not limited to a formal complaint filed by a coworker or guest, witnessed aggression, or a hostile confrontation — constitutes immediate grounds for termination of employment, without waiting for the July 16 review date.
