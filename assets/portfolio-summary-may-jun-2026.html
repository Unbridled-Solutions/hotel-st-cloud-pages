May–June 2026 · Hotel St. Cloud
🏆

Local Proximity is your engine — protect it at all costs. Near-identical spend both months ($2,431 vs $2,426), 22+ conversions each month, CTR improving from 19.9% → 21.5%. This campaign is running like clockwork. Don't touch it.

🚀

FP Sports Bar exploded in volume but not in value. Impressions jumped +259% in June (8,679 → 31,117) after the bid optimizations — that's the bid work paying off. But conversions stayed flat at 1/month both months. We've opened the faucet; the pipe still isn't converting. The issue is landing page or offer alignment, not traffic.

⚠️

Royal Gorge Basecamp conversion drop needs attention. More impressions (+33%), more clicks (+49%), but conversions fell off a cliff — 9 in May, 3 in June. The traffic is arriving, it's just not booking. Worth a look at whether the landing page experience changed or if the shift was seasonal mix (activity vs. lodging searchers).

📈

Standard Oil is on a positive trajectory. First conversion in June, clicks more than doubled, spend scaled from $86 → $189. Small numbers but the direction is right. Keep the budget steady and let it grow.

💡

The one thing to fix right now: FP Sports Bar conversions. You're spending ~$1,050/month and getting 1 conversion. The traffic is there. Audit the FP landing page — does it have a clear call to action? Is there a reason to show up today (happy hour, game night)? A single strong offer on that page could turn this from a cost center into a performer.
