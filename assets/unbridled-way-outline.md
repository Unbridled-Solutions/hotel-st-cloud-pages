## Format Notes
- Each Part: story opener (human, personal, visceral) → chapter cases (universal, applicable)
- Reflection questions: max 3 per chapter, honest and thought-provoking
- Photos: real people/moments blended with modern design elements
- Faith-informed but not Bible-forward — scripture lives in the appendix
- Voice: plain English, Stan's cadence, shorter is better, clever never cheesy
