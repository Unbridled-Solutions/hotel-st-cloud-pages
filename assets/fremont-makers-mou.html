Section 2
Purpose

This Memorandum of Understanding sets forth the agreed terms for the relaunch of Fremont Makers LLC, a Colorado limited liability company, effective August 1, 2025. The parties intend to formalize these terms in a fully executed Operating Agreement, Loan Agreement, and Commercial Lease prepared by legal counsel.

Fremont Makers LLC will engage in cabinet sales, woodworking, light manufacturing, framing, and related trades. All prior company assets — including woodshop equipment currently financed through FCA — are acknowledged as existing assets of the entity and are incorporated into the relaunched company.
