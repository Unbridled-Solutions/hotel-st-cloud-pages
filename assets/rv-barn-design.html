Design Specification — Preliminary

Log RV Storage Barn

Prepared for TJ's Log & Timber — estimating and fabrication review
Client: Stan Bullis Date: May 2026 Revision: 3 — All items confirmed
Building Specifications — All Confirmed
Footprint
20 ft wide × 40 ft deep (800 sq ft). The 20 ft wall is the front face — garage door wall. The 40 ft dimension is the depth running back from the front.
Log Size
12-inch diameter full-round logs.
Log Style
Chinked construction. Chinking compound between all log courses. Match chinking color to existing home.
Log Species
Match existing home — honey/amber tone. Confirm species with TJ's (likely Western Red Cedar, Engelmann Spruce, or Lodgepole Pine).
Garage Door
12 ft high × 16 ft wide, centered on the 20 ft front wall. Overhead sectional door. Leaves approximately 2 ft of log wall on each side of the door.
Personnel Door
36" door, rear wall, right side. Standard entry door.
Windows
None. Interior lighting via electrical only.
Roof Style
Gable roof — ridge runs the full 40 ft depth (front to back). Pitch to match existing home (est. 8:12–10:12). Dark charcoal/navy standing seam metal to match existing residence. Roofline to align with adjacent attached garage.
Wall Height
Minimum 14 ft sidewall to accommodate 12 ft door + header + top plate logs. Manufacturer to confirm based on log course stacking.
Foundation
Slab on grade — monolithic concrete. Coordinate with local contractor per code.
Interior
Open bay — no partition walls. Electrical by separate contractor after structure is set.
Site
Immediately to the left of the attached two-car garage on existing log home. Colorado high country, lake setting. Barn must read as unified compound with the residence.
