Design Specification — Preliminary

Log RV Storage Barn

Prepared for TJ's Log & Timber — estimating and fabrication review
Client: Stan Bullis Date: May 2026 Revision: 2 — All items confirmed
Building Specifications — All Confirmed
Footprint
20 ft wide × 40 ft deep (800 sq ft). The 20 ft wall is the front face — garage door wall. The 40 ft dimension runs back from the front.
Log Size
12-inch diameter full-round logs.
Log Style
Chinked construction. Chinking compound between all log courses. Match chinking color to existing home.
Log Species
Match existing home — honey/amber tone. Confirm species with TJ's (likely Western Red Cedar, Engelmann Spruce, or Lodgepole Pine).
Garage Door
12 ft high × 16 ft wide, centered on the 20 ft front wall. Overhead sectional door (roll-up or sectional). Leaves approximately 2 ft of log wall on each side.
Personnel Door
36" door, rear wall, right side. Standard entry door, no sidelights.
Windows
None. Interior lighting via electrical only.
Roof Style
Gable roof — pitch to match existing home (est. 8:12–10:12 per site photo). Dark charcoal/navy standing seam metal matching existing residence. Ridge runs along the 40 ft length. Align roofline with adjacent attached garage.
Wall Height
Minimum 14 ft sidewall to accommodate 12 ft door + header + top plate logs. Manufacturer to confirm based on log course stacking.
Foundation
Slab on grade — monolithic concrete. Coordinate thickness and reinforcement with local contractor per code.
Interior
Open bay — no partition walls. Electrical rough-in (lighting, outlets) by separate contractor after structure is set.
Site
Sited immediately to the left of the attached two-car garage on existing log home. Colorado high country — lake setting, mountain backdrop. Barn must read as unified compound with existing structure.
