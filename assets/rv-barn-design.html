Building Specifications — All Confirmed
Footprint
20 ft wide × 40 ft deep (800 sq ft). The 20 ft wall is the front face — garage door wall. The 40 ft dimension is the depth.
Log Size
12-inch diameter full-round logs.
Log Style
Chinked construction. Match chinking color to existing home.
Log Species
Match existing home — honey/amber tone. Confirm species with TJ's.
Garage Door
12 ft high × 16 ft wide, centered on the 20 ft front wall. Overhead sectional door. ~2 ft of log wall on each side.
Personnel Door
36" door on the right side wall (40 ft wall), near the rear corner. Approximately 2 ft from the rear wall.
Windows
None.
Roof
Gable roof — ridge runs front to back (along the 40 ft length). Pitch to match existing home (est. 8:12–10:12). Dark charcoal/navy standing seam metal.
Wall Height
Minimum 14 ft sidewall to clear 12 ft garage door + header. Confirm with manufacturer.
Foundation
Slab on grade — monolithic concrete.
Interior
Open bay. Electrical by separate contractor.
Site
Immediately left of attached garage on existing log home. Colorado high country, lake setting.
