Create Your Agent's Telegram Bot

It takes about 5 minutes. You only do it once.

1

Download Telegram (if you haven't already)

  • Download Telegram free at telegram.org or from the App Store / Google Play.
  • Create an account with your phone number.
  • Set a username: tap the menu (☰) → Settings → Edit Profile → Username. This is how your agent will identify you.
2

Open BotFather

  • In Telegram, search for "@BotFather" (the official blue checkmark bot).
  • Tap Start.
3

Create your bot

  • Type /newbot and hit send.
  • BotFather will ask for a display name — this is what shows up in your chat (e.g. "Max" or "Aria").
  • Then it will ask for a username — this must end in "bot" (e.g. "MaxAtAcmeCo_bot"). This is permanent, so choose carefully.
  • BotFather will give you a token that looks like: 8673964185:AAEW-bpYDGzfa1Ir2FJtWvx-kUPkT1HgXNI
4

Send us your token

  • Copy the full token BotFather gave you.
  • Send it to your BeUnbridled onboarding contact (or reply to your onboarding email).
  • Keep it safe — treat it like a password.

⚠️ Keep your bot token private. Don't share it publicly or commit it to code.

That's it. We'll take it from here and have your agent live within 24 hours.
